Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

A kinder school, commonly referred to as kindergarten, is an educational institution designed for young children, typically between the ages of 4 and 6.

Elementary School

An elementary school is an educational institution that provides foundational education to children, typically ranging from ages 6 to 12, covering grades 1 through 5 or 6.

Middle (10-16) Years

A middle school is an educational institution that serves students typically between the ages of 11 and 14, covering grades 6 through 8, though this can vary by region.
